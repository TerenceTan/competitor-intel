Phase 2 cannot finalize the per-market scraper loop or the `/markets/[code]` Digital Presence section without this list being correct.

Output: A corrected `src/lib/markets.ts` whose `PRIORITY_MARKETS` array, `MARKET_NAMES` record, and `MARKET_FLAGS` record all use the same 8 keys, in the same order, matching CONTEXT.md D2-01.
